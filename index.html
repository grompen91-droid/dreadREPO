About the Mod

Horror that lives
in the walls.

R.E.P.O. is already tense. Dread makes it suffocating. Four interlocking systems combine into something that feels like the game was always supposed to play this way.

Tension System

One scan.
Four responses.

01

Adrenaline

Sprint energy drains up to 70% slower when an enemy is within 15m. Scales linearly with distance. Fully restored on scene transition.

02

Panic Sprint

Sprinting near an enemy triggers a 1.25× speed burst for 2 seconds. 20s cooldown. Uses Traverse to access the private SprintSpeedMultiplier field.

03

Out of Breath

Plays a gasping breath clip when stamina drops below 5 after sprinting. 60s cooldown. Supports breath2.ogg and breath3.ogg variants.

04

Fake Footsteps

Every 3–6 minutes, a 20% chance to spawn a 3D footstep sound 2.5–5m behind you. Pitch randomized 0.5×–1.5×. No source. Nothing is there.

Every 60–180 seconds a clip is selected by weighted random draw, spawned at a random 3D position 5–15m from camera, pitch rolled 0.5×–1.5×. The AudioSource self-destructs after playback.
